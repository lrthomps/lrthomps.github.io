<p>Studied math and physics, now a data scientist, in Vancouver, BC.</p>

<p>In Vancouver because I love the mountains so near, prefer my snow up there, don’t mind the rain down here, and could not live in an un-walkable/un-bikeable place.</p>

<p>Machine learning because it felt natural after physics, closer to what’s important in everyday life, exciting in how powerful it can be, challenging in how badly it can fail.</p>

<p>Data Science because it combines math, programming, and problem solving always in context of what is <em>really</em> important, be that the real world, a constrained infrastructure, or a fickle market.</p>
